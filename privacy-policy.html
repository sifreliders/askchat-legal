We use information to:

  • Create, authenticate, and manage user accounts
  • Provide AI chat, image analysis, PDF analysis, translation, and image generation features
  • Send prompts, uploaded files, images, or other submitted content to AI service providers to generate responses
  • Provide generated AI outputs
  • Manage free usage, credits, limits, and rewarded ad access
  • Verify subscriptions and provide premium access

  • Prevent abuse, fraud, spam, and security issues

AskChat uses third-party AI and cloud service providers to provide AI-powered features.

When you use AI features, the text, images, files, PDFs, URLs, or other content you submit may be sent to third-party service providers for processing. These providers may include, but are not limited to:

  • OpenAI
  • Runware
  • Google services
  • Firebase
  • Other configured AI, translation, moderation, or cloud service providers

These providers process submitted content to generate responses, analyze images or files, translate text, moderate content, or provide related AI functionality.

Do not submit confidential, private, sensitive, or highly personal content unless you understand that it may be processed by third-party service providers.

AI-generated responses may be inaccurate, incomplete, or inappropriate. You should not rely on AskChat for professional legal, medical, financial, or other high-risk advice.

Uploaded content may be processed temporarily by third-party providers to provide AI features. Retention practices may vary depending on the third-party provider and the specific service used.

9. Account and Data Deletion

If you sign in to AskChat using Google Sign-In or create an account in the app, you can delete your account and request deletion of associated account data.

You can delete your account inside the AskChat app by opening the app menu, going to Account, and selecting Delete Account.

You can also request account and data deletion by visiting our account deletion page: https://sifreliders.github.io/askchat-legal/account-deletion.html

When your account deletion request is processed, AskChat will delete your Firebase account and main AskChat account data associated with your account. This may include account identifiers, sign-in related profile information, stored chat history, generated content history, usage records, and app credit records where applicable.

Some limited information may be retained where required or permitted for legal, billing, security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, safety, tax, compliance, or legitimate business reasons.

If you need help with account or data deletion, you may contact us at: sifreliders@gmail.com

Please note that deleting your AskChat account does not automatically cancel an active Google Play subscription. Subscriptions must be cancelled through Google Play.
